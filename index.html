Frequently asked questions

  • Your billing cycle begins on the day that you pay for your subscription and lasts 30 days.
  • Payment made and account still inactive - Forward the confirmation message to our customer support team for billing correction.

  • You can effect an upgrade at the end of your billing cycle or at any time by paying the full amount of the desired higher speed package and then notifying our customer care service to effect the upgrade

  • If you would like to switch to a lower priced package you will pay the desired amount and select the package you desire on the app and the service will begin.

  • If you would like to get connected to our network its simple. Contact our customer care contact anytime and book an installation. In case you already own a router from a previous provider you only have to pay for an internet packages of your choice. No installation fee

  • If you are moving house or would like your router to be moved to a different location, contact customer support to assist in the service. Relocation services are billed separately at a rate of Ksh. 1000.
